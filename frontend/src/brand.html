Brand Guidelines Summary

Cinder Logo

  • Layered circles represent warmth, analysis
  • Organic, ember-like feeling
  • Main product identity
  • Use for company branding

Bit Logo

  • Offset squares represent precision, AI
  • Digital, geometric feeling
  • AI assistant identity
  • Use for chat, analysis features

Usage Rules

  • Always maintain proper proportions
  • Minimum size: 16px for favicons
  • Use original colors when possible
  • Test readability at small sizes

Brand Relationship

  • Complementary, not competing
  • Circles vs squares = organic vs digital
  • Same color family creates unity
  • Perfect for "Bit by Cinder" hierarchy
